Privacy Policy

Last updated July 23, 2026

Overview

This site (the "Service") is a personal portfolio. It's a static site with no accounts, forms, or e-commerce, so it doesn't collect personal information like your name, email, or address. The only data collected is anonymous usage data via Google Analytics, described below.

Cookies & Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use it: which pages are viewed, how long visitors stay, and general location and device information. Google Analytics uses cookies to do this.

Analytics cookies are off by default. They're only set after you click "Accept" on the cookie banner. You can withdraw consent at any time via the "Cookie Preferences" link in the footer of any page, which will stop future tracking and clear your choice.

You can also opt out of Google Analytics across all sites that use it by installing Google's Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

What This Site Doesn't Do

This site has no contact forms, newsletter signups, or logins, so it never asks you for personal information directly. It doesn't sell or share data with advertisers, and it doesn't use any tracking or advertising cookies beyond the analytics cookies described above.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics is provided by Google LLC. You can read Google's own privacy practices in the Google Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

This site does not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date above.
